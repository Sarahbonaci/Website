About

Sarah bonaci (born1999) is a multi media artist based in Malta. She holds a diploma in design studies and is currently under going a BFA in digital arts. Using mainly 35mm film photography and polaroid instant film her work explores topics such as impermanence, memory and time. Elements of performance are incorporated and various methods of destruction are explored to symbolize aspects of human nature.
